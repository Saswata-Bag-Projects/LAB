About Mr. Saswata Bag:

Mr. Saswata Bag is a passionate innovator specializing in embedded systems, IoT, and bio-sensing technologies. His groundbreaking work in industrial safety and environmental monitoring has earned him multiple prestigious awards.

Expertise & Technical Skills:

Embedded Systems (Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi), IoT & Wireless Communication (GSM, GPS, RF Modules), Sensor Integration (MQ series, MAX30102, MG811), Hardware Prototyping, Programming (C, C++, Python), Data Logging & Analysis (SD Card Modules, RTC DS3231).

Achievements & Awards:

1st Prize in Disaster Management - Rajya Stariya Bal Vaigyanik Pradarshani (2024), 1st Prize - Research & Industrial Conclave Integration '24 (RIC), 1st Prize - Environmental Hackathon 2024 at IIT Guwahati (IITG), Best Theme - India International Science Festival (IISF).

Notable Projects:

Chetak (Smart Coal Mine Safety System), Smart Safety Helmet (Environmental Monitoring), EMG-Controlled Wheelchair (Hands-Free Mobility), Smart Bin (Automated Waste Management), Tetris on Arduino (LED-based Tetris Game).
