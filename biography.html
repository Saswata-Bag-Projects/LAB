About Mr. Saswata Bag

Mr. Saswata Bag is an innovative technologist and researcher specializing in **embedded systems**, **IoT**, and **bio-sensing technologies**. With a deep-rooted passion for problem-solving, he has successfully led numerous award-winning projects aimed at improving industrial safety and environmental sustainability.

  • 📡 **IoT & Wireless Communication**: GSM, GPS, RF Modules
  • 🧪 **Sensor Integration**: Gas Sensors (MQ series, MG811), Pulse Oximeters (MAX30102), EMG sensors
  • 🔧 **Hardware Prototyping**: Circuit design, PCB prototyping, and system integration

🔬 Notable Projects

1. Chetak – Smart Coal Mine Safety System

A robust monitoring and alert system designed for **coal mines**, incorporating real-time gas detection (Methane, CO, CO₂, Hydrogen), temperature monitoring, and data logging. This project enhances worker safety and automates emergency alerts.

2. Smart Safety Helmet

An advanced helmet with real-time **shock detection** and environmental monitoring to protect workers in hazardous environments.

3. EMG-Controlled Wheelchair

A mobility-assist device that interprets **EMG (Electromyography)** signals for hands-free control, empowering individuals with physical disabilities.

4. Smart Bin

An IoT-based **automated waste management system** that detects bin capacity and sends SMS alerts when the bin is full, optimizing waste collection.

5. Tetris on Arduino (Ardui No)

A creative recreation of the classic **Tetris game** using LED strips, a joystick, and an Arduino Mega.
