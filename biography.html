About Mr. Saswata Bag

Mr. Saswata Bag is a passionate innovator specializing in **embedded systems**, **IoT**, and **bio-sensing** technologies. His groundbreaking work in **industrial safety** and **environmental monitoring** has earned him multiple prestigious awards.

Expertise & Technical Skills

  • 🚀 **Embedded Systems**: Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi
  • 📡 **IoT & Wireless Communication**: GSM, GPS, RF Modules
  • 🧪 **Sensor Integration**: Gas Sensors (MQ series, MG811), Pulse Oximeters (MAX30102)
  • 🔧 **Hardware Prototyping**: Circuit design, PCB prototyping
  • 💻 **Programming**: C, C++, Python (for embedded systems)
  • 📊 **Data Logging & Analysis**: SD Card Modules, RTC (DS3231)

🏆 Achievements & Awards

  • 🥇 **1st Prize in Disaster Management** – Rajya Stariya Bal Vaigyanik Pradarshani (2024)
  • 🥇 **1st Prize** – Research & Industrial Conclave Integration '24 (RIC)
  • 🥇 **1st Prize** – Environmental Hackathon 2024 at IIT Guwahati (IITG)
  • 🏅 **Best Theme** – India International Science Festival (IISF)

🔬 Notable Projects

1. Chetak – Smart Coal Mine Safety System

An advanced monitoring system for **coal mines** integrating real-time gas detection, temperature monitoring, and automated alerts.

2. Smart Safety Helmet

A safety-enhancing helmet with **shock detection** and environmental monitoring for hazardous work environments.

3. EMG-Controlled Wheelchair

A mobility aid using **EMG signals** to provide hands-free control for physically challenged individuals.

4. Smart Bin

An IoT-based **waste management system** that automatically detects bin fullness and sends SMS alerts for collection.

5. Tetris on Arduino (Ardui No)

A recreation of the classic **Tetris game** using LED strips and an Arduino Mega.
