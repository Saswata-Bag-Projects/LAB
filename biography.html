Biography of Saswata Bag

An Innovator Specializing in Embedded Systems, IoT, and Environmental Monitoring Technologies.

About Me

I am Saswata Bag, a passionate researcher and innovator with expertise in developing cutting-edge solutions for industrial safety and environmental monitoring. My work focuses on building smart systems using Arduino, IoT, and sensor technology to address real-world challenges.

Expertise & Technical Skills

  • Embedded Systems: Arduino Uno, Mega, Nano, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi.
  • IoT & Communication: GSM (SIM900A), GPS, RF modules, real-time alerts via SMS.
  • Sensor Integration: Gas sensors (MQ-4, MQ-7, MQ-8, MG811), DHT11, MAX30102 (pulse oximeter).
  • Programming: C, C++, Python for data processing and hardware control.
  • Data Logging: RTC (DS3231) for real-time tracking and SD card modules for long-term data storage.
  • System Prototyping: Hardware-software integration for real-time monitoring and control systems.
