Courses Offered

These courses are designed to provide in-depth knowledge in the fields of IoT, embedded systems, and industrial safety.

Core Courses

  • 1. Introduction to IoT and Embedded Systems
    A beginner-friendly course covering the fundamentals of Internet of Things (IoT), microcontrollers, and real-time applications.
  • 2. Advanced Sensor Technology and Applications
    In-depth exploration of modern sensor technologies and their applications in industry and research.
  • 3. Industrial Automation and Control Systems
    A practical approach to designing and implementing automated industrial solutions using Arduino and other microcontrollers.

Specialized Courses

  • 4. Smart Safety Systems in Hazardous Environments
    Focuses on developing and deploying safety monitoring systems for high-risk environments such as coal mines.
  • 5. Wireless Communication and IoT Protocols
    Detailed study of wireless technologies like GSM, LoRa, and MQTT, with real-world implementation in IoT devices.
  • 6. Assistive Technology and Healthcare Devices
    Designing medical and assistive devices, including EMG-controlled wheelchairs and health monitoring systems.

Workshops & Training Programs

  • 7. IoT for Beginners: Hands-On Workshop
    A practical workshop introducing IoT basics with hands-on projects using Arduino and sensor modules.
  • 8. Real-Time Monitoring with Arduino and Raspberry Pi
    Learn how to design and deploy monitoring systems for real-time applications using popular microcontrollers.
  • 9. Data Acquisition and Logging Systems
    Advanced training on implementing SD card modules and real-time data acquisition systems for industrial use.

Upcoming Courses

  • 10. AI and Machine Learning in IoT
    Applying AI techniques to enhance the capabilities of IoT systems for predictive analysis and automation.
  • 11. Advanced Robotics and Autonomous Systems
    Dive into autonomous navigation, robotic control, and AI integration in next-generation systems.
  • 12. Blockchain for IoT Security
    Securing IoT ecosystems using blockchain technology to enhance data integrity and communication.
